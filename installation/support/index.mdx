title: Remote Access
---

Setting up access for remote support is essential, as it allows Direct Energy Partners to assist with troubleshooting and provide help 
during the commissioning process. Follow the steps below to set up access for remote support using TeamViewer or Windows Quick Assist.

### TeamViewer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and Install TeamViewer">
    - Visit the [TeamViewer website](https://www.teamviewer.com/).
    - Click on the **Free Download** button in the top right corner.
    - Choose the **Free** option and click on **Download now**.
    - Select your operating system from the options provided (Windows, macOS, etc.).
    - Decide whether to install (either option will work):
      - **TeamViewer QuickSupport** (for instant support sessions)
      - **TeamViewer full client** (for full remote control and meeting functions)
    - Follow the on-screen instructions to download the appropriate installer for your system.
    - Locate the downloaded installer file on your computer and run it.
    - Follow the prompts provided by the installer to complete the installation process.
    - Once the installation is complete, open the TeamViewer application on your laptop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Laptop to the PPL Controller">
    - Use an Ethernet cable to connect your laptop directly to the PPL controller. The connection method varies depending on your PPL version:
      - **Basic and Premium Versions:** Connect your laptop directly to the PPL controller using one of the two Ethernet ports located at 
      the bottom of the controller.
      
      <img
        src="/images/phoenix_contactaxc_f_2152_ethernet.png"
        alt="Phoenix ContactAXC F 2152 Ethernet"
        style={{ width: '400px', margin: 'auto' }}
      />
      
      - **Pro Version (PPL Panel):** Connect your laptop to the Ethernet connection port at the bottom of the panel. This port is 
      directly connected to the network switch inside the PPL panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Credentials With DEP PPL Support Team">
    - Ensure your laptop is connected to the PPL controller and the TeamViewer application is running.
    - Locate your personal credentials in the TeamViewer application. These credentials include:
      - ID: `[9-digit or 10-digit code]`
      - Password: ****`[see TeamViewer]`+
      
      **TeamViewer Quick Support:**

      <img
        src="/images/teamviewer_quick_support.png"
        alt="TeamViewer Quick Support"
        style={{ width: '400px', margin: 'auto' }}
      />
      
      **TeamViewer Full Client:**
      
      <img
        src="/images/teamviewer_full_client.png"
        alt="TeamViewer Full Client"
        style={{ width: '400px', margin: 'auto' }}
      />
    
    - Send a request for remote support to [pplsupport@directenergypartners.com](mailto:pplsupport@directenergypartners.com) and be sure to include the credentials in the email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Windows Quick Assist

To use the built-in Quick Assist application on a Windows computer for remote support, follow these steps. Note that Quick Assist only works on Windows computers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quick Assist on Your Computer">
    - In the search bar located in the taskbar at the bottom of your screen, type **Quick Assist** and open the application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Computer to the PPL Controller">
    - Use an Ethernet cable to directly connect your computer to the PPL controller.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Remote Support">
    - Send an email to [pplsupport@directenergypartners.com](mailto:pplsupport@directenergypartners.com) requesting remote support.
    - The PPL support team will reply with a 6-digit code.
    - Enter the provided code into the Quick Assist app and click on **Submit**.
        
    <img
      src="/images/windows_quick_assist.png"
      alt="Windows Quick Assist"
      style={{ width: '400px', margin: 'auto' }}
    />
        
    - A connection will be established between your computer and the PPL support team's computer. This may take a few seconds.
    - Once the connection is established, the PPL support team will request remote control through the Quick Assist app. Click **Allow Control** in the top bar to grant the support team access to control your computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Additional Information

In the future, other methods to set up remote access may be added. For now, the steps above outline how to set up remote support using TeamViewer and Windows Quick Assist.
