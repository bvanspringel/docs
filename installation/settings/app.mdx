title: App Installation
---

To install the Power Platform app and other apps, follow the steps below:

### Install App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to the WBM">
    <Card
      title="Login to the WBM"
      icon="lock-open"
      href="/installation/settings/login"
      horizontal
    >
      Login to the Web-Based Management (WBM) interface of the Power Platform controller.
    </Card>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Administration Tab">
    Navigate to the **Administration** tab in the left navigation plane and select **PLCnext Apps**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install App">
    Click on **Install App** and upload the app you want to install on the Power Platform controller.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start App">
    Click on the **Start** button to start the Power Platform application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each app you want to install on the Power Platform controller.

### Required Apps

There are a few apps that are required to be installed on the Power Platform controller to ensure proper operation. These apps are the **Power Platform App** 
and the **Mosquitto MQTT Broker**.

Download the apps from the [PLCnext App Store](https://plcnext-appstore.com/) or click on the links below.

<Card
  title="Power Platform App"
  icon="download"
  href="https://plcnext-appstore.com/"
  horizontal
>
  Download the Power Platform App from the PLCnext App Store.
</Card>

<Card
  title="Mosquitto MQTT Broker"
  icon="download"
  href="https://www.plcnextstore.com/us/app/1597"
  horizontal
>
  Download the Mosquitto MQTT Broker from the PLCnext App Store.
</Card>

Make sure to download version **2.0.15** of the Mosquitto MQTT Broker.
